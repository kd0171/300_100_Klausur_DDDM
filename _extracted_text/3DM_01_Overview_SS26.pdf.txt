Summary
•
Service processes over time
•
New information (demand, requests, traffic, drivers, battery levels, requirements, etc.)
•
Reactions and updates of planning required
•
Reactions might be too late (vehicle stuck in traffic, no drivers around to deliver meals,
drone crashes, etc.)
•
Anticipation of an expected future required
•
Aim at flexibility with respect to a future system state

Decision making
•
Real-World: Actions are performed and
resulting states can be observed
•
Aggregation: Transfers real-world data
into dimensions of information model
•
Information Model: Depicts information in
a condensed way
•
Decision Model: Defines decision space
and evaluates decision
•
Implementation: Translates solution of the
decision model into real-world actions
